PERF
STARTUP -- GRAPHIC DESIGN

What is Perf?

Perf is a startup in Evanstan, Illinois that matches a perfume to people's personalities, making it an easy and enjoyable experience.

Illustrations and Animation

On the Perf website, there is a quiz that determines your perfume to your personality. As a Graphic Designer, my project was to create illustrations that align with the quiz answer choices, and also add animations as interactie rollovers.

Result

By creating illustrations, it give a visuals to people taking the quiz. The rollover animation maintains the users attention on the quiz, and creates a more delightful experience.
