PERSONAL PROJECT

What is the Ordinary?

The Ordinary is a affordable and transparent skin care brand sold mostly online under the company Deciem.



Identifying the Problem

The products are exceptional considering the price, but their online website is overwhelming. It is hard to navigate, and the product descriptions are hard to find. With the products labeled by their scientific names, it is hard to identify what the product actually does.

The brand requires necessary research for the customer to purchase the products for personal skin type and concern.



Creative Vision of the App

To solve the problem, I have brainstormed an app which allows for more customer interaction with the company.

Other than performing as an online market, the app includes the Match Studio where the user inputs information about their skin. With this information, the brand suggests products targeting different concerns, carefully explaining what the product does, and the directions of application.

The Match Studio strengthens user and brand interaction. The Ordinary app's overarching goal is to allow the user to be fully engaged and comfortable with the navigation and act as a digital dermatologist to match their skin to The Ordinary's products.
